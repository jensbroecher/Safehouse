About

SafeHouse Records is a multinational, multitalented and multiexceptional music production stable. With the likes of DJ Guv'na, MC Lawy, Royal Flush, MC El, The Phoenom and Triple-S, we make no exceptions when it comes to bringing out the best in what needs to be done. With musical influences from around the globe, ranging from Germany, Malaysia, Cameroon, South Africa, USA but to name a few, our style is that of universal appeal. We cater to nearly all genres of music and culture and are able to find a a link between the different sub-genres in the industry. Having worked with artists such as Ramzey Siele, Chiwawa, Jahmani, Mumbi and Hopiho, our record itself speaks for itself. At the studio we offer music and voice recording, mixing, mastering, instrumentals creation and music production. Out of the studio we also have SafeHouse branded t-shirts and caps designed to your satisfaction.

History

The idea behind SafeHouse Records was conceived in 2001, when an emerging DJ Duff teamed up with his longtime childhood friend MC Klein Sereto. They formed an unofficial duo of DJ and MC and, then youngsters fresh out of high school, began working together on just goofing about with freestyle mixtapes.

As the years went on, they spent more and more time in learning the ropes of the entertainment industry. DJ Duff started DJing at night clubs, while on the other side of the world MC Sereto was busy getting into rapping at clubs and parties. Every now and then, they would meet up and work on ways of gathering enough of the right equipment to start a studio of their own. The concept became known as Duff Entertainment.

During his tenure in Hamburg, Sereto, teamed up Petah Wesh and there, having gathered some reasonable equipment, set up a studio in his room. They began writing and recording songs and holding performances withing the neighbourhood. It was at this time that the idea of a name change to SafeHouse came about. This was due to the hostel resembling a former world war bunker. The two continued to do their recordings and then teamed up with rapper 4Rena and producer Junior Fuzah in order to form the stable Blue Corners and SafeHouse.

In 2011 SaheHouse Records was registered as a record label and entertainment business and was now ready to begin commercial production. With the now newly acquired equipment, Sereto returned home to set up the studio. Again teaming up with DJ Duff, who at this time had already become a well known dj on the local scene, they were ready to begin. Junior Fuzah followed shortly afterwards and the recording studio began operations. Lots of young talent was discovered in the likes of MC Lawy, MC El and The Phoenom among others. Working with these artistes was a blessing, but due to visa circumstances, producer Junior Fuzah had to return to Germany.

It was at this point that another long time friend and DJing prodigy of DJ Duff had returned from Malaysia, after spending 4 studying sound engineering and music production. His name was DJ Guv'na. He joined aboard the SafeHouse bandwagon and immediately brought some new light and talent onto the scene. Joining alongside DJ Guv'na were The Commisioner and Royal Flush, and together they formed the extraordinary production team that is SafeHouse Records.
